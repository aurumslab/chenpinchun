PROJECT BRIEF

This section introduces the project, its context, and the main goals.

Project Brief

Aurum Studio is a lo-fi music platform that combines design and music, blending aesthetics, sound, and interactive experiences to provide users with a more focused and immersive experience. This case study documents the entire process of Aurum Studio, from research and ideation to development.

MY CREATIVE JOURNEY

This section outlines the design process, covering the journey from idea exploration and research to the development of the final product.

My Creative Journey

The process began with a simple observation: lo-fi music has become a common tool for focus and relaxation, but existing platforms often lack immersive experiences and personalized features. To address this, I mapped out the creative journey:

  • Research existing Lo-Fi music platforms
  • Identify gaps between existing platforms and user needs by conducting user research.
  • Design user flows and wireframes based on user needs.
  • Create prototypes, conduct user testing, and refine the design based on feedback

EXPLORING USER NEEDS

This section outlines the user goals and pain points identified through user research.

Exploring User Needs

To design a Lo-Fi music platform that meets user needs, I researched user behaviors, goals, and pain points. Through this research, I aimed to develop a product that truly addresses user needs. The findings showed that users desired more personalized features while maintaining focus and an immersive experience, so my design principles are: simplicity, focus, and personalization.

PROBLEM & SOLUTION

This section clearly defines the core challenge and the approach taken to address it.

Problem & Solution

Through research, I identified the main pain points of existing Lo-Fi platforms and proposed corresponding solutions to enhance the user experience.

Problem 1

Existing platforms are full of ads, making it hard for users to stay focused.

Solution 1

Integrated a Pomodoro timer within the platform to help users focus without distractions.

Problem 2

Existing platforms lack an immersive experience and offer limited control over the listening environment.

Solution 2

Added multiple ambient sound options, allowing users to customize their listening environment.

Problem 3

Users have limited customization options on the platform, reducing engagement and personal connection.

Solution 3

Added playlist selection and theme-switching features, enabling users to personalize both their auditory and visual experience.

IDEATION & WIREFRAME

This section presents the product concept, exploration, and initial design process.

Ideation & Wireframe

I designed the interface and planned the user flow through sketches and low-fidelity wireframes. The following summarizes my design concepts:

  • A clean, distraction-free interface with focus as the core.
  • Integrated Pomodoro timer + customizable sound effects in different environments.
  • Two different themes allowing users to personalize their visual and auditory experience.

Extra Features:
Beyond the core focus and music functions, I also designed sections for collaboration and free support, enabling users to engage more deeply with the platform and supporting its long-term sustainability.

Redesign - Theme Toggle Button

To make theme switching more intuitive, I redesigned the toggle button by adjusting its appearance and style, changing it from a square button to an icon-based button. The update allows users to more easily identify the current theme, improving overall usability and aesthetics.

IMPACT & LEARNING

This section presents the project outcomes and the lessons learned throughout the design process.

Impact & Learning

This project demonstrates how combining music and design can help users maintain focus while creating an emotional connection. Key outcomes:

  • A dedicated Lo-Fi music platform bridging the gap between music streaming services and productivity tools.
  • Positive user feedback on personalization features, including themes, ambient sounds, and playlist selection.
  • Future opportunities: expanding community features (shared playlists), mobile app development, and AI-generated personalized mixes.

What I learned:

  • Users value simplicity but also meaningful personalization.
  • Design is not just about visuals â when paired with sound, it creates a richer immersive experiences.
  • User testing and iterative design are crucial for refining subtle but impactful details.
